4.6.3 Update note:

Personality System
- Only children, insane, or evil sims will be seen whistling. The sound echos to the player's screen if they are nearby.
- Turning around awkwardly will only happen with children and insane sims.
- Most interactions have been disabled for get to work workplaces, bars, clubs, restaurants, and cafes.
- Call to clean and call to bed has been modified. 
- Only clumsy sims, children, angry sims, or dazed/drunk sims will have a chance of falling.

All Systems
- Rabbit Holes fixed!
